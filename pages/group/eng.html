Group

AGESCI Sea Scout Group Catania 14 was founded on 1988 in Ognina Catania. Later the group moved to San Francesco di Paola Church in Civita, Catania, Sicily, Italy. Over more than 30 years our community acts with: Branco Popolo Libero (kids between 8 and 10), Reparto 20.000 Leghe (boys and girls between 11 and 15) and Clan Smile (boys and girls between 16 and 20).
